Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Posted On: 2025-09-20

Description:

A creamy, comforting Italian pasta dish with pancetta, egg, and Parmesan.

Categories:

"Italian", "Pasta", "Dinner"

Ingredients:

"12 oz spaghetti", "4 oz pancetta", "2 large eggs", "1/2 cup grated Parmesan", "Black pepper", "Salt"

Recipe:

"Cook spaghetti until al dente.", "Fry pancetta until crispy.", "Whisk eggs with Parmesan and pepper.", "Drain pasta and quickly toss with pancetta.", "Stir in egg mixture off heat to create a creamy sauce.", "Serve with extra Parmesan on top."
